[blog-to-podcast](https://bp.madelynn.blue/) is a website that converts blogs (feeds or individual pages) into a podcast.
I wanted this because there's some content I'd like to listen to and there's no podcast of it.
This site polls the subscriptions, uses a speech-to-text service and hands out a podcast feed with audio.
