[statsk.boo](https://statsk.boo) is a WFTDA StatsBook aggregator and browser.
Before this, the only way to see statsbooks for WFTDA (roller derby) sanctioned (competitive, ranked) games was to look in a big gdrive folder grouped per quarter.
The spreadsheets there are dense and hard to find.
This makes it way easier.
